About Us

What is Nila

"Nila is a community-driven association based in Liverpool, created to bring together nurses and healthcare professionals. We aim to foster a sense of unity and cultural enrichment among our members while providing a platform for growth and development."

Our Mission

"To celebrate diversity, promote professional camaraderie, and support the well-being of nurses and healthcare workers through cultural events, networking opportunities, and advocacy."

Our Vision

"To build a supportive, inclusive space that encourages both personal and professional growth for all our members."

Advocacy

Support, Empowerment, Representation

Supporting Healthcare Professionals

Nila is dedicated to advocating for fair work conditions, better mental health support, and a healthy work-life balance for all healthcare workers.

Campaigns and Initiatives

Workplace Safety Campaign: An initiative focused on creating safer work environments for nurses, reducing workplace hazards, and promoting awareness of safety protocols.

Healthcare Workers' Rights: Advocacy for policies that support nurses' rights, including fair wages, work hours, and access to healthcare benefits.
